Dashain Photo

Dashain, a major Hindu festival celebrated in Nepal and India, is a nine-night celebration that marks the triumph of good over evil. It is a time of family reunion, feasting, and religious rituals. The main theme of Dashain revolves around the worship of Goddess Durga, the Hindu goddess of power and protection. The festival is marked by various rituals, including the worship of the Goddess in different forms, animal sacrifices (in some regions), and the exchange of gifts and blessings. The final day of Dashain, known as Vijaya Dashami, is celebrated with great enthusiasm, symbolizing the victory of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura.
